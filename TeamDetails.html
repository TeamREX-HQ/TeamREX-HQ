About Us

Welcome to the REX Robotics Club! We are a group of passionate individuals dedicated to exploring the field of robotics. Our mission is to foster innovation, creativity, and collaboration among students and professionals in the field of robotics. Our team comprises 12 enthusiastic students from various engineering disciplines:

Mechanical Engineering (MECH):Vanshika Tomar, Gunika Tyagi, Shuruti Singh, Ayushi Kashyap, and Poorvi.

Technology (TECH): Shambhavi Sinha, Darakhshan, Shubh Krishna, and Suryanshi.

Electronics and Communication Engineering(ECE):Mahita Boyina, Anjali Kumari, and Sheetal Rana.

Each member contributes their expertise to build durable robots, develop advanced software, and integrate essential electronics. Together, we are driven by a shared passion for robotics, pushing the boundaries of what is possible and creating innovative solutions. Join us on our journey to make a significant impact in the world of robotics!
