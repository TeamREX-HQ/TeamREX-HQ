REX

We are limitless...

REX is an all-female team composed of 12 talented members who collaborate on innovative projects within the fields of STEM, particularly focusing on robotics. The team is organized into different departments, including tech, mechanical engineering, and electronics and communication engineering. Together, they work on cutting-edge projects, showcasing their expertise in design, electronics, and software development, while pushing the boundaries of technology.

What We Do ?

REX focuses on designing, developing, and implementing cutting-edge solutions, blending robust mechanical design, sophisticated electronics, and advanced software to create impactful and efficient technological solutions.

Who Are We?

Our club is made up of enthusiastic students from various disciplines, all connected by a shared passion for robotics and technology. From coders and engineers to designers and strategists, we bring together a diverse set of skills and viewpoints to create innovative projects that have a meaningful impact.
