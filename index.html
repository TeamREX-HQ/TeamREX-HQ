About-Us

Vision

To be a leading student-led organization that empowers the next generation of engineers by fostering creativity, teamwork, and hands-on learning opportunities.

Goals

Innovation in Engineering: Develop cutting-edge, sustainable, and efficient rovers for various terrains and applications, pushing the boundaries of technology.

Skill Development: Provide team members with hands-on experience in design, manufacturing, and automation engineering.

Collaboration and Teamwork: Foster a collaborative environment where students from diverse disciplines can contribute their skills to a common project.

Competition Success: Compete in national and international competitions, showcasing our expertise in building human-powered rovers and other engineering projects.

Promote STEM Education: Inspire future engineers and innovators through outreach programs and partnerships with educational institutions

Mission

To provide a dynamic and collaborative environment where students can gain practical engineering experience, enhance their technical skills, and work on innovative projects that prepare them to become future leaders in technology and engineering.
